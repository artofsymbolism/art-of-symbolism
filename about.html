About Our Mission

At Art of Symbolism, we believe there’s more to the world than what meets the 👁—and we’re here to uncover it. Our mission is to develop an AI vision model that dives beneath the surface, revealing hidden symbols and stories often overlooked or dismissed.

We take a community-driven approach, using decentralized platforms to gather insights from those who perceive the world differently. Every AI model carries some degree of bias, but ours embraces it—not to follow mainstream narratives, but to uncover truths that others may be reluctant to explore. Our dataset, built through contributions on X, includes images of public figures and scenes that some might label as "conspiracy" but which we see as revealing deeper realities.

Through our cryptocurrency initiative, we’re connecting a global network of contributors. By sharing unique content with us, you’ll help us train our AI on unconventional datasets, free from the influence of “woke(blind🗿) culture.” Together, we’re pushing boundaries, and offering a fresh perspective on the world around us.
